# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Assume good faith. Keep discussions focused on the work.

## What we expect

- Treat other contributors with respect, regardless of experience level, identity, or background.
- Critique ideas, not people. Code review is about the code.
- Acknowledge that people bring different perspectives and trade-off preferences.
- Help newcomers find their footing.

## What's not OK

- Personal attacks, harassment, or sustained disruption of discussion
- Sharing other people's private information without consent
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- Spam, off-topic self-promotion, or low-effort drive-by comments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will warn or ban participants who violate this code. Serious or repeated violations result in immediate removal.

To report a concern, email the project owner directly through GitHub. Reports are confidential.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source norms and is intentionally short. The goal is a productive working environment, not a legal document.
